How to install
  1. Put the downloaded .zip into your .minecraft/resourcepacks/ folder.
  2. In Minecraft: Options → Resource Packs, move it to the enabled side, click Done.
  3. The camera stream’s “off” screen now shows your animation/image. No live camera needed to see it.

Runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded. GIF frame extraction needs the ImageDecoder API (Chrome / Edge / recent Firefox); if your browser lacks it, convert the GIF to MP4 first. Declares both the old (pack_format/supported_formats) and new (min_format/max_format, 25w31a+) format fields, so it stays valid on Minecraft 1.21.8 and newer.
